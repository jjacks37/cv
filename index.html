Jasmine Jackson

Illustrator and Designer

Hi! My name is Jasmine Jackson. I was raised in Riverdale, Georgia. I am currently a student at Savannah College of Art and Design and I’m getting my B.F.A. in Illustration. I can’t remember the time when I was first introduced to art, but I do know I’ve always liked drawing and coloring. I think it was when I was in the 8th grade when I decided to seriously try to train myself to get better at drawing. I would take the sketchbook my parents had bought me for Christmas and sit for hours in front of the computer looking at images and trying to replicate them. The first art teacher I had that taught me about the foundations of art was my 10th grade art teacher, Mr. Norwood.
My art work now is based on portraits of people. I’m trying to capture their likeness but also tell the viewer a bit about their personality through my use of color and other little details. I start my and finish my work in photoshop. I use textured brushes so that the pieces don’t look too digital. I like the rough look of traditional so sometimes I scan in a sketch that I’ve done and start to paint on top of that.
